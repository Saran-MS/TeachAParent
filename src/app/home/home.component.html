What is Teach a Parent?

Teach a Parent is a webinar series, where the students conduct the session to teach the elderly. The webinar series covers a series of topics related to Technology, which might be helpful for parents to understand the developing world in a better way.

Currently, it is organized with the help of students from Coimbatore. We have plans to implement it nation-wide in the future.
